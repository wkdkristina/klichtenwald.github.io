<h2>Content & Strategy Deep-Dives</h2>
<p><b>Content Lifecycle & Workflow Engineering</b></p>
<p>Scaling from four articles a month to 40 requires more than just writers; it requires a system. I design end-to-end workflows that eliminate bottlenecks and ensure every asset is optimized.</p>
<p>I do this by using custom content operations boards that make the hand off between teams seamless. The implementation of "SEO Gates" prevents a post from moving to published unless it meets specific technical and on-page criteria.</p>
<p>All this increases the publication velocity by 30-50% while reducing the manual cleanup time needed by the SEO team after drafts are created.</p>

<p><b>Topical Authority & "Moat" Building Strategy</b></p>
<p>Google no longer ranks keywords, it ranks expertise. I move companies from 'random acts of content' toward topical clusters that build an unshakeable competitive moat.</p>
<p>Using Competitive Gap Analysis, I indentify where the competition is thin. I then map out pillar and cluster architectures that link high intent 'money pages' to educational 'top of funnel' content, signaling to search engines that the brand is the undisputed authority in the space.</p>
<p>This strategy doesn't just win one keyword; it wins the entire category, making it harder for competitors to displace the brand even with higher ad spend.</p>

<p><b>Technical-Editorial Liason & SOP Development</b></p>
<p>The biggest leak in most companies is the friction between the creative (writers) and the technical (Devs). I act as the translator between these two worlds.</p>
<p>I create non-technical SOPs for editorial teams. These are simple, actionable guides that teach writers how to handle complex tasks like "Semantic Header Hierarchy" and "Image Alt-Text Optimization" without needing a computer science degree.</p>
<p>This fosters a culture of SEO across the entire organization. When the writers and the developers understand the why behind the technical requirements, the quality of the output rises across the board.</p>

<p><b>Resrouce Orchestration & Team Management</b></p>
<p>Managing a $20,000/month SEO budget requires the same level of scrutiny as a technical audit. I ensure the company gets an optimized return on every external dollar spent.</p>
<p>I don't just oversee the process; I audit the deliverables against business KPIs. I identify low-value bloat in contracts, like generic monthly reporting, and redirect those funds toward high-impact projects like Core Web Vitals sprints or link earning campaigns.</p>
<p>This ensures the marketing budget is lean and high impact, protecting the company from "SEO busywork" and focusing exclusively on activities that drive MRR and pipeline.</p>

<h3 style="margin-top: 40px;">The Editorial Guardrails</h3>
<p>I implement standardized scoring models to ensure every piece of content meets strict SEO and brand requirements before it reaches the "Ready to Publish" stage.</p>
